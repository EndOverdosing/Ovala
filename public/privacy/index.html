Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental. We are committed to protecting it.

Data Collection

We do not store or log the websites you visit or any data you submit through the proxy. All traffic is processed in-memory and discarded immediately. No personal information is required to use this service.

Analytics

This site may use minimal, anonymous analytics to track overall service usage (e.g., number of visitors), but this does not track your specific browsing activity through the proxy. This helps us understand server load and improve the service.

Cookies & Third Parties

Be aware that while we protect your privacy on our end, the websites you visit can still track you with cookies and other technologies. Using a proxy does not make you completely anonymous online. Ovola does not set any tracking cookies of its own.
